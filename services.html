CNC Milling

CNC milling is a subtractive manufacturing process that involves using specialized cutting tools to remove material from a solid block to create a desired shape or object. CNC milling services use computer-controlled machines to produce precise and intricate parts for various industries, including aerospace, automotive, and medical devices. The process is highly automated and efficient, making it suitable for producing high volumes of complex parts with consistent quality.

Lathe

CNC Lathe services involve using specialized machines to produce complex and precise parts by rotating a workpiece while cutting tools remove material to create the desired shape. The process is highly automated and controlled by computer software, ensuring consistent quality and high accuracy. CNC Lathe services are widely used in various industries, including aerospace, automotive, and medical devices, for producing parts such as shafts, cylinders, and threaded components.
